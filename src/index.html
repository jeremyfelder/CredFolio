An Attested Educational Profile


EdFolio makes it easier for students to share their educational data with universities and companies. From grades to research to certifications, their accomplishments are all in one place, attested to by the institutions themselves.

How does it work? When a student takes a test, the academic institution will upload their score to the student's profile. The uploaded data is saved on the Ethereum Blockchain with a timestamp proving the data was uploaded by the institution to the student's profile at a given time. The student can only see the scores that have been uploaded to their profile but cannot change or add to their educational data themselves. Only the student and the institution that uploaded the data can see it, unless the student decideds to share it with another institution.
